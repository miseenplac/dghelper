DungKey Tracker

RS3 Dungeoneering key/door tracker.
Click the button below to install this app into Alt1.

Add to Alt1

Before your first dungeon

  1. Open the plugin in Alt1, click Settings in the header.
  2. Enter Your name (your in-game RSN) in the first field.
  3. Under Calibration, click Calibrate next to Ring of Kinship and follow the two-hover prompts (hover top-left corner, then bottom-right).
  4. Do the same for DG Map.
  5. Close the Settings panel with Done.
Important: the Ring of Kinship party interface must be open while you're in a floor. RS3 does not re-open it automatically when you enter a new floor from Daemonheim \u2014 open it once (right-click the Ring of Kinship accessory \u2192 Party) and leave it open. Without this panel visible, the plugin can't auto-detect your slot colour or teammates' names, and multi-party door pinning on the DG map will not work.
If this page isn't opened inside Alt1's built-in browser the button won't work.
